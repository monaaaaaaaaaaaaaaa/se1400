Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

It will contain examples of art pieces the person I am creating this portfolio for (Referred to as 'Hen' for privacy's sake).

Where will you get all of the content for your site (images/text)?

I will get all of the images and the like from Hen herself.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care because the portfolio will provide several examples of what Hen can create, potentially convincing them to commission art from her.

Why should a site be built for this?

It is an easy way of providing an art portfolio in the case that someone may be interested in commissioning art from her and wants to look at some examples of her art style.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to explore the site and use it as an assisting factor in deciding whether or not they would like to commission art from Hen.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal is to provide example pieces of art that Hen has created and wants to show off as examples of what she can create.
